## Overview

The Nazgul DC5 ECO is a 5-inch freestyle drone that redefines performance and value for money. Equipped with the DJI O4 Digital HD system, it delivers razor-sharp, propeller-free 4K video even in low light conditions. The robust carbon frame and aluminum camera mount guarantee durability without sacrificing lightness. The pre-installed GoPro mount makes the drone even more versatile.

Thanks to the BLITZ ATF435 flight controller and the powerful BLITZ E55S ESC, the Nazgul DC5 ECO offers a reliable and dynamic flying experience. With a maximum speed of 190 km/h and an optimized DeadCat design, this drone is perfect for FPV pilots looking for performance and quality.

---

## Features

- **4K Ultra HD video**: DJI O4 system with 1/1.3-inch sensor for stunning footage and stable 155° ultra-wide angle video
- **Robust and lightweight**: High-quality carbon frame with aluminum components for a stable construction
- **Anti-Spark technology**: Protects the electronics from power surges and extends the life of the XT60 connectors
- **Optimized DeadCat design**: No disturbing propellers in the camera image - ideal for professional aerial photography
- **Easy maintenance**: Built-in signaling devices and easily accessible components ensure convenient handling
